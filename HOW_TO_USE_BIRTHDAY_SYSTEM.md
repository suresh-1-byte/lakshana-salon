# 🎂 How to Use the Birthday Management System

## 📖 Quick Start Guide

This guide shows you exactly how to use the new Birthday Management System at Lakshana Beauty Salon.

---

## 👨‍💼 For Admin Staff

### Step 1: Access the Birthday Dashboard

1. **Open your browser** (Chrome, Safari, Edge, etc.)

2. **Go to Admin Panel**:
   ```
   https://lakshanabeautysalon.vercel.app/admin
   ```

3. **Login**:
   - Email: `admin@lakshanasalon.com`
   - Password: `Admin@123`

4. **Find Birthday Management**:
   - Look at the left sidebar
   - Find the **🎂 Birthday Management** menu item
   - Click it

---

### Step 2: View the Dashboard

Once you open Birthday Management, you'll see:

#### 📊 Statistics at the Top:
- **Total Customers**: How many customers have birthdays saved
- **Birthdays Today**: Critical! Send wishes immediately
- **Next 7 Days**: Plan ahead for upcoming birthdays

#### 🔍 Search Bar:
- Type customer name or mobile number
- Results filter in real-time
- Helpful when you have many birthdays

#### 🎉 Today's Birthdays Section:
- **Special pink cards** - these are priority!
- Shows customer name, phone, email
- "Birthday Today!" badge
- **Action needed**: Send wishes today

#### 📅 Upcoming Birthdays Section:
- Shows next 7 days
- Displays exact date and countdown
- "Tomorrow" or "In X days" labels
- Plan your outreach

---

### Step 3: Send Birthday Offer via WhatsApp

#### For Each Customer:

1. **Find the customer** in the birthday list

2. **Click the green button**:
   - "Send WhatsApp Offer" (for today's birthdays)
   - "Send Offer" (for upcoming birthdays)

3. **WhatsApp will open automatically**:
   - On mobile: Opens WhatsApp app
   - On desktop: Opens WhatsApp Web

4. **You'll see a pre-filled message**:
   ```
   Hi [Customer Name] 🎉🎂
   
   Your birthday is today! 🥳
   
   We have a special birthday offer exclusively for you 🎁✨
   
   🎁 Birthday Special Offer:
   ✨ 20% OFF on all services
   🌸 Complimentary hair spa
   💅 Free nail art design
   
   [... rest of message ...]
   ```

5. **Review the message**:
   - Check customer name is correct
   - Personalize if needed
   - Add any extra details

6. **Click Send in WhatsApp**

7. **Done!** ✅

---

### Step 4: Refresh the Dashboard

- Click the **"Refresh"** button at the top right
- This updates the birthday list
- Helpful after sending multiple wishes
- Auto-refreshes when you navigate to the page

---

## 👥 For Customers (How They Provide Birthday)

### During Booking:

1. **Customer visits website**

2. **Scrolls to "Book Your Experience" section**

3. **Fills the booking form**:
   - Name (required)
   - Phone (required)
   - Email (required)
   - **Date of Birth** (OPTIONAL)
     - Motivation: "Get special birthday offers! 🎂"
     - Easy date picker
     - Cannot select future dates

4. **Selects services**

5. **Submits booking**

6. **Birthday is saved automatically!**
   - Stored in customer profile
   - Used for future birthday wishes
   - Never asked again on repeat bookings

✅ **Check daily in the morning**
   - Open Birthday Management page

   - Send wishes before noon

✅ **Plan ahead**

## ❓ Common Questions

### Q: What if customer didn't provide birthday?

**A**: No problem! The system only shows customers who provided their birthday. You can:
- Ask for birthday during their next visit
- Add it manually to their customer profile
- They'll appear in future birthday lists

---

### Q: Can I customize the message?

**A**: Yes! When WhatsApp opens:
1. Message is pre-filled
2. You can edit before sending
3. Add personal touches
4. Change discount if needed

**Note**: Changes only apply to that one message. To change the default message for everyone, contact your developer.

---

### Q: Does WhatsApp send automatically?

**A**: No! This is manual sending:
1. You click button
2. WhatsApp opens
3. You review message
4. **You click send in WhatsApp**

This gives you control and prevents spam.

---

### Q: What if I don't see any birthdays?

**Possible reasons**:
- No customers have birthdays in next 7 days
- No customers provided DOB yet
- Search filter is active (clear it)

**Solution**:
- Click "Refresh" button
- Clear search box
- Check again tomorrow

---

### Q: Is this free or does it cost money?

**A**: 100% FREE! 🆓
- No API subscriptions
- No monthly costs
- No hidden fees
- Uses free WhatsApp web links

---

### Q: Works on both mobile and desktop?

**A**: Yes! Works perfectly on:
