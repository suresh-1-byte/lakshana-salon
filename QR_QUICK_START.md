# QR Review System - Quick Start Guide

## ✨ What's New?

Your admin panel now has a **QR Code Review System** that lets customers leave reviews by simply scanning a QR code!

## 🚀 How to Get Started (3 Easy Steps)

### Step 1: Generate Your QR Code
1. Go to **Admin Panel** → **Reviews**
2. Click the **"QR Code"** button (next to "Add Review")
3. A beautiful QR code will be generated instantly

### Step 2: Download & Print
1. In the QR modal, click **"Download QR"**
2. Print the QR code (recommended size: 4" x 4")
3. Place it at your:
   - Reception desk
   - Checkout counter
   - Service areas
   - On receipts

### Step 3: Manage Reviews
1. Customer scans QR → Fills form → Submits
2. Review appears in **"Pending"** tab
3. You click ✓ to **approve** (shows on website)
4. You click 👑 to **feature** (shows in testimonials carousel)

## 📱 What Customers See

When they scan the QR code:
1. Beautiful branded review page opens
2. They fill in:
   - Name ✓
   - Phone (optional)
   - Star rating ✓
   - Review text ✓
   - Service name (optional)
3. Submit → Get thank you message
4. Review waits for your approval

## 🛡️ Important Notes

- All reviews start as **"Pending"** - you must approve them
- Only **approved + featured** reviews show in website carousel
- You can **reject** or **delete** inappropriate reviews
- Phone numbers are **optional** for privacy
- Reviews are tracked by source (QR, manual, website, etc.)

## 📊 Review Management

**Filter Options:**
- **All** - See everything
- **Pending** - New submissions waiting for approval
- **Approved** - Visible on website
- **Rejected** - Not shown

**Actions:**
- ✓ **Approve** - Make public
- ✗ **Reject** - Hide from public
- 👑 **Feature** - Show in testimonials
- 🗑️ **Delete** - Remove permanently

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Ask for reviews right after service** - Happiness is highest!
2. **Train staff** to show QR code and explain it's quick (30 seconds)
3. **Multiple locations** - Place QR code in 3-4 spots
4. **Follow up** - Send review link via WhatsApp after visit
5. **Respond to reviews** - Feature the best ones prominently
