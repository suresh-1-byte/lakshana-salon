# **App Name**: Lakshana Premiere

## Core Features:

- AI Visual Consultation Tool: An intelligent beauty advisor tool that uses generative AI to recommend specific treatments and makeup palettes based on user-provided style goals and facial descriptions.
- Executive Service Catalog: An interactive, elegant grid showcasing premium services like Bridal Makeovers and Hair Spa treatments with detailed information and high-fidelity visuals.
- Luxury Booking Concierge: A seamless, intuitive multi-step reservation form allowing clients to select preferred specialists, dates, and times for an personalized experience.
- Masonry Transformation Gallery: A sophisticated visual showcase with light-box functionality and hover interactions to highlight bridal transformations and professional styling.
- Brand Milestone Ledger: A clean section utilizing motion graphics to display business statistics, including experience years and total client reach, to build immediate credibility.
- Client Voice Carousel: An auto-scrolling testimonial slider featuring high-praise client reviews and professional star ratings with sleek micro-interactions.
- Integrated Atelier Map: An embedded, custom-styled Google Maps component paired with business details to guide high-end clientele to the salon's physical location.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Luxury Champagne Gold (#D4AF37) for headlines and high-intent call-to-action buttons.
- Background color: Deep Onyx (#0B0B0B), a heavily desaturated variant of the primary hue providing a high-contrast dark scheme.
- Accent color: Sophisticated Rose Gold (#B76E79) for secondary decorative elements and borders to soften the palette.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', an elegant high-contrast serif for an high-end, editorial fashion feel. Body font: 'Inter', a modern sans-serif for neutral, clear readability.
- Use Lucide React to implement minimalist, ultra-thin line icons that match the sophistication of high-fashion branding.
- An editorial two-column hero design and balanced 3-column service grid inspired by high-end boutique hotel digital presences.
- Leverage Framer Motion for subtle fade-up reveals, elegant hover elevations, and gentle parallax scaling for salon imagery.
