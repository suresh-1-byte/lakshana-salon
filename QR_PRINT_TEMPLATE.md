## 🎨 Design Guidelines

### Colors
- **Primary:** #D4447A (Pink)
- **Secondary:** #B03060 (Dark Pink)
- **Background:** White or #FFF0F5 (Light Pink)
- **Text:** #2D1B25 (Dark)

### Fonts
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond (elegant)
- **Body:** Inter or similar sans-serif
- **QR Code:** Always have white space around it

### QR Code Specifications
- **Format:** PNG
- **Background:** White
- **Color:** Pink (#D4447A)
- **Quiet Zone:** At least 0.25" border around QR
- **Error Correction:** High (in case of damage)

## 📐 Sizing Guidelines

| Use Case          | QR Size  | Total Print | Distance |
|-------------------|----------|-------------|----------|
| Table Tent        | 4" x 4"  | 5" x 7"    | 1-3 feet |
| Wall Poster       | 6" x 6"  | 8.5" x 11" | 3-6 feet |
| Receipt           | 1.5" x 1.5" | 2" x 3"  | 6-12 inches |
| Standee           | 8" x 8"  | 24" x 36"  | 6-10 feet |

## 🖨️ Printing Instructions

### For Professional Printing:
1. **File Format:** Save as PNG at 300 DPI
2. **Color Mode:** CMYK for print, RGB for digital
3. **Paper:** Glossy or matte cardstock (250gsm+)
4. **Lamination:** Recommended for durability
5. **Bleed:** Add 0.125" bleed if required

### For Home Printing:
1. Use color printer (not black & white)
2. Use thick paper or cardstock
3. Print at highest quality setting
4. Test scan before mass printing

## ✅ Final Checklist Before Printing

- [ ] QR code scans correctly on multiple devices
- [ ] URL leads to correct review page
- [ ] Brand colors match your salon
- [ ] Text is legible from intended distance
- [ ] White space around QR code (quiet zone)
- [ ] Contact info included (optional)
- [ ] Print quality is 300 DPI or higher
- [ ] Test print one copy first
- [ ] Check for typos in text

## 🎯 Placement Strategy

**Week 1:** Start with 2-3 locations
- Reception desk (table tent)
- Main service area (wall poster)
- Checkout counter (small card)

**Week 2:** Analyze which locations get most scans

**Week 3:** Expand to high-performing locations

**Ongoing:** Update designs seasonally

---

**Pro Tip:** Include a small incentive like "Leave a review and get 10% off next visit" to increase participation! 🎁
